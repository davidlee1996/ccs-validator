# Related Resources

## Government Tools You Can Leverage

### CCSI Toolset (DOE/NETL)
The Carbon Capture Simulation Initiative Toolset is a suite of computational models for carbon capture equipment [citation:3]. CCS-Validator aims to complement these tools by adding validation-focused testing layers.

- **FOQUS**: Framework for Optimization and Quantification of Uncertainty and Surrogates [citation:10]
- **SimSinter**: Connects Aspen Plus simulations to FOQUS [citation:4]
- **Process Models**: MEA solvent systems, adsorption models [citation:7]

### Academic Research
- Aspen Plus + Brightway integration for LCA [citation:1]
- Multi-scale modeling approaches from NETL's CCSI2 project [citation:6]
